Sheryl Rouse

Name: Sheryl Rouse Crawford
Stage Name: Sheryl Rouse
Hometown: LaGrange, North Carolina
Current Home: Louisville, Ky.
Number of Years in Louisville: 17 years

Sheryl Rouse is one of the most talented and energetic performers in the world. She was born in LaGrange, North Carolina and she is now a resident of Louisville, Kentucky. She relocated to Kentucky in 2001 with her wonderful husband and three, beautiful children. She has been singing and entertaining audiences since the tender age of three. She has been compared to the likes of Gladys Knight, Patti LaBelle, and Aretha Franklin and has performed with artists such as: Al Green, Eddie LeVert, Shirley Murdock, Frankie Beverly, Lenny Williams, and so many others all across the United States. She was a contestant on the world-famous, Showtime At the Apollo in New York City. Sheryl was victorious and defeated all of her opponents. She has played the lead acting role in several stage plays including: God Blocked It, Ain't Misbehaving, Bubblin' Brown Sugar, Grease, Dreamgirls, The Wiz, Porgy and Bess, and A Good Man Is Hard To Find. Sheryl's purpose in life is to touch souls through her music. She gives God all of the praise for her continued success throughout her career. She performs regularly and always delivers top, quality entertainment.

She currently performs at Headliners Music Hall, The Levee River House, Stevie Ray's Blues Bar and many other venues, publicly and privately. Sheryl is also working with producers and musicians in Louisville, Ky. and California to release her very own CD, while continuing to perform in beautiful venues all over the tri-state area. She is the "Songbird from the South" and has no plans of slowing down. She is still living her dream!
